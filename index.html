How It Works

Getting started with PawCare is quick, seamless, and completely guided.

1

Download & Setup

Grab the app from our site and create your personal account. Then, set up your pet's customized profile with their name, breed, and photo.

2

Connect Your Feeder

Follow the simple in-app instructions to pair your ESP32-powered automated feeder to your home Wi-Fi and link it directly to your account.

3

Set Schedules & Records

Create automated feeding schedules that work even when offline. Input past veterinary records, upcoming vaccinations, and set health reminders.

4

Monitor in Real-Time

Enjoy complete peace of mind. Use the dynamic dashboard to track feed logs, review health charts, or dispense food instantly via the "Feed Now" remote control.
