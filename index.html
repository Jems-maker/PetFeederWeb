Workflow

Getting started with PawCare is quick, seamless, and completely guided.

STEP 01

Download & Setup

Grab the app from our site, create your personal account, and set up your pet's customized profile with their photo.

STEP 02

Connect Hardware

Follow the simple in-app instructions to pair your ESP32-powered feeder to your home Wi-Fi directly.

STEP 03

Set Schedules

Create automated feeding schedules that work even when offline. Input past veterinary records.

STEP 04

Real-Time Monitor

Use the dynamic dashboard to track feed logs, review health charts, or dispense food instantly.
